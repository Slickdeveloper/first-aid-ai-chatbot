Title: Seizures
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/seizures
Summary: Paraphrased seizure first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross seizure resource.

During a seizure, do not hold the person down or try to stop their movements.

Move objects away that could cause injury and monitor the person.

If possible, turn the person onto their side without causing injury.

Call 9-1-1 if the seizure lasts longer than 5 minutes, more than one seizure happens in a row, the seizure happened in water, there is an injury, or it is the person's first seizure.

After the seizure, if the person is unresponsive but breathing, place them in the recovery position and continue to monitor them.
