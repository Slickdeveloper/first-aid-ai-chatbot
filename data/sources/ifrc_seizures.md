Title: Seizures
Organization: IFRC
Source URL: https://www.ifrc.org/document/international-first-aid-resuscitation-and-education-guidelines
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased seizure first-aid guidance from the IFRC International First Aid, Resuscitation and Education Guidelines.
Protect the person from nearby hazards during the seizure.

Do not restrain their movements and do not put anything in the mouth.

When the seizure stops, if possible and safe, place the person on their side and keep monitoring breathing and responsiveness.

Call emergency services if the seizure lasts longer than 5 minutes, repeats, causes injury, or if it is the person's first seizure.
