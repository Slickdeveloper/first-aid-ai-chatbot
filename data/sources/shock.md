Title: Shock
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/shock
Summary: Paraphrased shock first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross shock resource.

Call 9-1-1 immediately if you suspect shock.

Lay the person on their back unless that position causes discomfort or trouble breathing.

Keep the person from getting cold or overheated and give care for the condition causing the shock, such as controlling severe bleeding.

Do not give anything to eat or drink, and stay with the person while you keep checking breathing and responsiveness.
