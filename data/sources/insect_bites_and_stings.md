Title: Insect Bites and Stings
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/insect-bites-and-stings
Summary: Paraphrased first-aid guidance for insect bites and stings based on the American Red Cross resource.

Remove the stinger as quickly as possible if one is still in the skin.

Wash the area with soap and water.

Apply a cold pack wrapped in a thin towel to help reduce pain and swelling.

Watch for signs of a severe allergic reaction, such as trouble breathing, swelling, or shock.

If there are signs of anaphylaxis, assist with epinephrine if available and call emergency services immediately.
