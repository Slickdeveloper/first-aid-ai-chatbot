Title: AED Steps
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/aed/using-an-aed/aed-steps
Summary: Paraphrased AED-use guidance based on the American Red Cross AED steps page.

If the person is unresponsive and not breathing normally, call for emergency help and bring the AED to the person as quickly as possible.

Turn on the AED right away and follow the voice prompts.

Expose the chest, dry it if needed, and attach the pads as shown on the AED diagrams.

Make sure no one is touching the person while the AED analyzes the heart rhythm, and stay clear if the AED tells you to deliver a shock.

After the shock, or if no shock is advised, begin CPR immediately and continue following the AED prompts until the person recovers or trained help takes over.
