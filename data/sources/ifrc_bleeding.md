Title: Severe Bleeding
Organization: IFRC
Source URL: https://www.ifrc.org/document/international-first-aid-resuscitation-and-education-guidelines
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased severe-bleeding guidance from the IFRC International First Aid, Resuscitation and Education Guidelines.
Apply direct pressure to severe bleeding immediately.

If possible, use a dressing or cloth and maintain firm pressure without delay.

If bleeding is life-threatening and does not stop, follow local guidance for tourniquet use if you are trained and it is appropriate.

Call emergency services and keep watching for signs of shock while waiting for help.
