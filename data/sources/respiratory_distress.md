Title: Respiratory Distress
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/respiratory-distress-trouble-breathing
Summary: Paraphrased trouble-breathing guidance based on the American Red Cross respiratory distress resource.

Trouble breathing can include shortness of breath, gasping for breath, breathing that is faster and shallower than normal, or breathing that is uncomfortable or painful.

Call 9-1-1 if the person has severe trouble breathing, becomes unresponsive, or their condition gets worse.

Help the person rest in a position that makes breathing easier and loosen tight clothing.

If they use prescribed breathing medication, help them take it if appropriate and within your training.

Keep watching for changes in breathing and responsiveness until emergency help arrives.
