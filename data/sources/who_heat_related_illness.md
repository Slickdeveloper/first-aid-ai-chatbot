Title: Heat-Related Illness
Organization: World Health Organization
Source URL: https://www.who.int/health-topics/heatwaves/during-a-heatwave-help-others-who-suffer-from-the-heat
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased heat-related first-aid guidance from WHO heatwave health guidance.
Move the person to a cool place and loosen or remove excess clothing.

Cool the body with cool water, cool cloths, fanning, or other available cooling methods.

If the person is conscious and able to drink, give cool water or other fluids according to local guidance.

Heat stroke is a medical emergency, so call emergency services immediately if the person becomes confused, collapses, loses consciousness, or has very hot skin.
