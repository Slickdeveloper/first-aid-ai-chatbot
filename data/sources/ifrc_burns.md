Title: Burns
Organization: IFRC
Source URL: https://www.ifrc.org/document/international-first-aid-resuscitation-and-education-guidelines
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased burn first-aid guidance from the IFRC International First Aid, Resuscitation and Education Guidelines.
Cool the burn with cool or lukewarm running water as soon as possible.

Continue cooling long enough to reduce heat in the tissues, while avoiding ice or anything that may cause cold injury.

Remove rings, watches, or tight items near the burn if they are not stuck to the skin and if it is safe to do so.

Cover the burn with a clean dressing or suitable covering and seek medical care for large burns, deep burns, facial burns, or breathing problems.
