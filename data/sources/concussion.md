Title: Concussion
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/concussion
Summary: Paraphrased concussion first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross concussion resource.

Have the person stop the activity they were doing and rest without moving.

Leave them in the position found while waiting for emergency help if serious symptoms are present.

Watch for confusion, vomiting, worsening headache, behavior changes, breathing changes, or reduced responsiveness.

If 9-1-1 was not needed right away, the person should still follow up with a healthcare provider for a full evaluation.
