Title: Animal Bites
Organization: World Health Organization
Source URL: https://www.who.int/news-room/fact-sheets/detail/animal-bites
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased animal-bite first-aid guidance from the WHO animal bites fact sheet.
If a person is bitten or scratched by an animal, begin wound care early.

Wash and irrigate the wound thoroughly with soap and running water.

Seek prompt medical care because infection prevention, rabies post-exposure care, or tetanus vaccination may be needed depending on the animal, the wound, and local risk.

If the bite causes severe bleeding or major injury, call emergency services immediately while giving appropriate first aid.
