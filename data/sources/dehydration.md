Title: Dehydration
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/dehydration
Summary: Paraphrased dehydration first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross dehydration resource.

Replace fluids with water, oral rehydration solution, sports drinks, or popsicles as appropriate.

Offer small, frequent amounts of fluid and gradually increase the amount as tolerated.

Keep the person in a cool place and continue watching for worsening symptoms.

Call 9-1-1 for signs of shock, inability to keep fluids down, severe decreased urination, persistent severe abdominal pain, or worsening responsiveness.
