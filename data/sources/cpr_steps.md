Title: CPR Steps
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/cpr/performing-cpr/cpr-steps
Summary: Paraphrased CPR guidance based on the American Red Cross adult CPR steps page.

If the person is unresponsive and is not breathing or is only gasping, call for emergency help immediately and get an AED if one is available.

Place the person on their back on a firm, flat surface and kneel beside them.

Give chest compressions in the center of the chest at a rate of 100 to 120 per minute, pressing hard and fast and allowing the chest to return to its normal position after each compression.

If you are trained and willing, give cycles of 30 compressions and 2 rescue breaths, making sure each breath lasts about 1 second and makes the chest rise.

Continue CPR and use an AED as soon as it is available, minimizing interruptions until the person shows signs of life or trained help takes over.
