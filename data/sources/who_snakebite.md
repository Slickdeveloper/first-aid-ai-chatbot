Title: Snakebite Envenoming
Organization: World Health Organization
Source URL: https://www.who.int/news-room/questions-and-answers/item/snakebite-envenoming
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased snakebite first-aid guidance from the WHO snakebite envenoming questions and answers page.
Keep the bitten person as still as possible and limit movement.

Do not use narrow tourniquets, cut the wound, apply chemicals, or attempt dangerous suction methods because these are ineffective or harmful.

In some settings, pressure at the bite site or a compression bandage may be used only according to local guidance for certain predominantly neurotoxic bites, and not for bites that cause major local tissue damage.

Transport the person to medical care without delay and, if possible, place them on their side to help protect the airway and breathing.
