Title: Eye Injury
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/eye-injury
Summary: Paraphrased eye-injury first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross eye injury resource.

For a chemical splash, flush the affected eye gently with clean water and keep the affected eye lower than the unaffected eye while flushing.

For an embedded object or splinter, do not try to remove it and seek immediate medical care.

Watch for blurred vision, loss of vision, continued pain, or bleeding in the eye, and call 9-1-1 if the injury is severe.
