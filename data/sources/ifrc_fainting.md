Title: Fainting
Organization: IFRC
Source URL: https://www.ifrc.org/document/international-first-aid-resuscitation-and-education-guidelines
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased fainting guidance from the IFRC International First Aid, Resuscitation and Education Guidelines.
If the person feels faint, help them lie down in a safe position and loosen tight clothing.

Watch their breathing and responsiveness closely.

If the person does not recover quickly, or if they become unresponsive, call emergency services and provide further care according to training.

If they are unresponsive and not breathing normally, begin CPR according to local guidance and your level of training.
