Title: Anaphylaxis
Organization: IFRC
Source URL: https://www.ifrc.org/document/international-first-aid-resuscitation-and-education-guidelines
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased anaphylaxis guidance from the IFRC International First Aid, Resuscitation and Education Guidelines.
Recognize anaphylaxis as a severe allergic reaction that may include swelling, breathing difficulty, wheezing, or shock.

Call emergency services immediately.

If the person has a prescribed epinephrine auto-injector and it is appropriate within your training and local guidance, help them use it without delay.

Keep the person under close observation, monitor breathing and responsiveness, and be prepared to give further care if the condition worsens.
