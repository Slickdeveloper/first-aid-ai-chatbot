Title: Diabetic Emergencies
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/diabetic-emergencies
Summary: Paraphrased diabetic-emergency first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross diabetic emergencies resource.

If the person is awake and able to swallow safely, give a source of sugar such as glucose tablets, juice, milk, or sugary candy.

If available, encourage use of prescribed glucagon by someone trained to give it.

Watch closely for changes in responsiveness, breathing, or signs that the person is getting worse.

Call 9-1-1 for trouble breathing, seizures, unresponsiveness, or if the person cannot safely swallow.
