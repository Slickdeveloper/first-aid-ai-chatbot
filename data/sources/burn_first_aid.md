Title: Burn First Aid
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/burns
Summary: Paraphrased burn first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross burns resource.

For a minor burn, cool the burned area under cool running water for at least 20 minutes as soon as possible.

Remove rings, watches, or tight clothing near the burn before swelling begins, but do not remove anything stuck to the skin.

Cover the burn loosely with clean cling film or a sterile, non-fluffy dressing.

Do not apply ice, butter, toothpaste, or oily creams to the burn.

Seek urgent medical help for burns on the face, hands, feet, genitals, very large burns, or if the person has trouble breathing.
