Title: Heat Exhaustion
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/heat-exhaustion
Summary: Paraphrased heat-exhaustion first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross heat exhaustion resource.

Move the person to a cooler place with circulating air.

Loosen or remove extra clothing, apply cool wet cloths or cool water to the skin, and fan the person.

If the person is responsive and able to swallow, have them slowly sip a cool drink with electrolytes and carbohydrates if available; offer water if not.

Call 9-1-1 if the person cannot take fluids, vomits, becomes less responsive, or does not improve.
