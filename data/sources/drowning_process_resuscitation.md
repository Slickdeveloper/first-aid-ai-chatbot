Title: Drowning Process Resuscitation
Organization: American Red Cross Guidelines Database
Source URL: https://guidelines.redcross.org/guidelines-database/drowning-process-resuscitation/
Summary: Paraphrased drowning resuscitation guidance based on the American Red Cross Guidelines Database.

If the person is out of the water and you determine they are in cardiac arrest, begin resuscitation right away.

Open the airway and give 2 rescue breaths first.

Continue CPR with cycles of 30 compressions and 2 rescue breaths if you are trained to do so.

Call emergency services and continue care until help takes over or the person shows signs of life.
