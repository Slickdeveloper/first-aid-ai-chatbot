Title: Nosebleeds
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/nosebleeds
Summary: Paraphrased nosebleed care guidance based on the American Red Cross nosebleed resource.

Have the person sit upright with the head slightly forward.

Pinch the nostrils shut for at least 5 minutes without checking too early.

If the bleeding has not stopped, pinch again for another 5 minutes.

If the bleeding is severe or gushing, call 9-1-1.

Keeping the head slightly forward helps prevent blood from pooling in the throat.
