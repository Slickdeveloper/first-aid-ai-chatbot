Title: Hypothermia
Organization: IFRC
Source URL: https://www.ifrc.org/document/international-first-aid-resuscitation-and-education-guidelines
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased hypothermia guidance from the IFRC International First Aid, Resuscitation and Education Guidelines.
Treat the person gently, move them away from the cold, and remove wet clothing if possible.

Insulate the person with dry clothing, blankets, or other suitable coverings to reduce further heat loss.

If the person is not shivering or needs more warming and you are far from medical care, use careful active rewarming methods without putting heat directly on the skin.

Call emergency services and keep checking airway, breathing, and circulation.
