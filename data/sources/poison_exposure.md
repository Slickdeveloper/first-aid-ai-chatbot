Title: Poison Exposure
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/poison-exposure
Summary: Paraphrased poison-exposure first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross poison exposure resource.

Move the person away from the poison source if you can do so safely.

If the person is alert and responsive, call Poison Help at 1-800-222-1222 and follow the instructions you are given.

Do not make the person vomit unless Poison Help or a medical professional specifically tells you to do so.

Call 9-1-1 for trouble breathing, seizures, major changes in responsiveness, or other severe symptoms.

If the person is unresponsive and not breathing, begin CPR according to your training. If the person is breathing but not fully awake, place them in the recovery position.

Try to identify what the poison was, how much was taken, and when it happened so you can share that information.
