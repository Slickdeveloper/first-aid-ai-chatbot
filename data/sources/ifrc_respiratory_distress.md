Title: Respiratory Distress
Organization: IFRC
Source URL: https://www.ifrc.org/document/international-first-aid-resuscitation-and-education-guidelines
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased respiratory-distress guidance from the IFRC International First Aid, Resuscitation and Education Guidelines.
Help the person rest in a position that makes breathing easier and loosen tight clothing.

Keep watching for changes in breathing, responsiveness, and any signs that the condition is getting worse.

If the person has prescribed breathing medication and it is appropriate within your training, help them use it according to local guidance.

Call emergency services immediately for severe breathing difficulty, worsening condition, or unresponsiveness.
