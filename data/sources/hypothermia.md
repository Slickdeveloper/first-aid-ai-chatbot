Title: Hypothermia
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/hypothermia
Summary: Paraphrased hypothermia first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross hypothermia resource.

Move the person to a warmer place and rewarm them slowly.

Remove wet clothes, dry the person, help them put on dry clothing, and wrap them in dry blankets or plastic sheeting if available.

Cover the head and use warm heating sources carefully if far from medical care, protecting the skin with a dry cloth.

If the person is alert and can swallow, offer small sips of a warm, non-caffeinated drink, and call 9-1-1 for severe symptoms such as confusion, unresponsiveness, or slow or no breathing.
