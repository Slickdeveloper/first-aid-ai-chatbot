Title: Burns First Aid
Organization: World Health Organization
Source URL: https://www.who.int/news-room/fact-sheets/detail/burns/
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased burn first-aid guidance from the WHO burns fact sheet.
Stop the burning process by removing smouldering clothing if it is safe to do so and by cooling the burn.

Use cool running water to reduce the temperature of the burn.

For chemical burns, irrigate with large volumes of water to remove or dilute the chemical.

Wrap the person in a clean cloth or sheet and transport them to an appropriate medical facility, while avoiding ice, oils, paste, or materials that may contaminate the wound.
