Title: Unresponsive and Breathing Person
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/unresponsive-and-breathing-person
Summary: Paraphrased care guidance for an unresponsive but breathing person based on the American Red Cross resource.

Check for responsiveness, breathing, and other life-threatening conditions for no more than 10 seconds.

If the person is unresponsive but breathing, call 9-1-1 and place them in the recovery position unless a head, neck, or back injury makes movement unsafe.

Keep the person from getting too cold or too hot and continue watching for any change in breathing or responsiveness.

If the person stops breathing or is only gasping, begin CPR and use an AED if available.
