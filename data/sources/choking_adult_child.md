Title: Adult and Child Choking
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/adult-child-choking
Summary: Paraphrased first-aid steps for adult and child choking based on the American Red Cross choking resource.

If a person cannot cough, speak, or breathe, call 9-1-1 or have someone do so immediately.

Give 5 back blows between the shoulder blades, then 5 abdominal thrusts, repeating the cycle until the object is forced out or the person becomes unresponsive.

If the person becomes unresponsive, begin CPR according to your level of training and check the mouth for an object before giving breaths.

For pregnant persons or people who are too large for abdominal thrusts, use chest thrusts instead.
