Title: Asthma Attack
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/asthma-attack
Summary: Paraphrased asthma-attack first-aid guidance based on the American Red Cross asthma attack resource.

Help the person sit up straight in a position that makes breathing easier.

Assist with or administer quick-relief medication if you are trained and it is prescribed for the person.

Help them use the inhaler as directed by their healthcare provider, and keep watching for changes in breathing and responsiveness.

Call 9-1-1 if the person has severe trouble breathing, cannot speak normally, gets worse, or does not improve after medication.
