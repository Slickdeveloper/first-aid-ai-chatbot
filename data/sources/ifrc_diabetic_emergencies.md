Title: Diabetic Emergencies
Organization: IFRC
Source URL: https://www.ifrc.org/document/international-first-aid-resuscitation-and-education-guidelines
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased diabetic-emergency guidance from the IFRC International First Aid, Resuscitation and Education Guidelines.
If the person is awake and able to swallow safely, give a glucose or sugar-containing food or drink according to local guidance and your training.

Keep watching for changes in responsiveness, breathing, or worsening symptoms.

If the person becomes unresponsive, has a seizure, or cannot safely swallow, call emergency services immediately.

Be prepared to give further care, including CPR, if the person's condition worsens.
