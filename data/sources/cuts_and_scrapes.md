Title: Cuts and Scrapes
Organization: Mayo Clinic
Source URL: https://www.mayoclinic.org/first-aid/first-aid-cuts/basics/art-20056711
Summary: Paraphrased first-aid guidance for minor cuts and scrapes based on Mayo Clinic guidance.

Wash your hands before caring for the wound.

Stop minor bleeding with gentle direct pressure if needed.

Rinse the wound with water, wash around it with soap, remove visible dirt if you can do so safely, and apply a thin layer of antibiotic ointment or petroleum jelly.

Cover the wound with a clean dressing and change it if it becomes wet or dirty.
