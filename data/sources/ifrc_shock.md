Title: Shock
Organization: IFRC
Source URL: https://www.ifrc.org/document/international-first-aid-resuscitation-and-education-guidelines
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased shock first-aid guidance from the IFRC International First Aid, Resuscitation and Education Guidelines.
Call emergency services if you suspect shock.

Help the person lie down in a safe position and keep them still unless movement is needed for safety or to provide care.

Keep the person from getting too cold or too hot and give care for the cause, such as controlling severe bleeding.

Do not give food or drink, and keep checking breathing, responsiveness, and other signs of worsening condition.
