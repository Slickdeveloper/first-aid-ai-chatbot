Title: Bruise First Aid
Organization: Mayo Clinic
Source URL: https://www.mayoclinic.org/first-aid/first-aid-bruise/basics/art-20056663?p=1
Summary: Paraphrased guidance for managing a bruise based on Mayo Clinic first-aid advice.

If possible, elevate the bruised area above heart level.

Apply an ice pack wrapped in a thin towel for about 20 minutes at a time.

Repeat cooling several times during the first day or two to help reduce pain and swelling.

If the bruised area is swelling, use an elastic bandage for gentle compression, but do not wrap it too tightly.

Seek medical care for very painful swelling, bruises that appear without a clear cause, unusual bleeding, a lump that forms over the bruise, or pain that does not improve.
