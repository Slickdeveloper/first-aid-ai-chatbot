Title: Fainting
Organization: American Red Cross
Source URL: https://www.redcross.org/take-a-class/resources/learn-first-aid/fainting
Summary: Paraphrased fainting care guidance based on the American Red Cross fainting resource.

Check the person for responsiveness and breathing.

If the person is unresponsive and not breathing, start CPR according to your training and use an AED if available.

If the person is breathing but not fully responsive, call 9-1-1 and place them in the recovery position if there are no injuries that would make movement unsafe.

If the person starts to feel faint but is still responsive, have them lie down, loosen tight clothing, and keep checking them.

Call 9-1-1 if symptoms do not improve quickly or if they happen again.
