Title: Choking
Organization: IFRC
Source URL: https://www.ifrc.org/document/international-first-aid-resuscitation-and-education-guidelines
Summary: Extracted and paraphrased choking guidance from the IFRC International First Aid, Resuscitation and Education Guidelines.
If a person cannot cough, speak, or breathe, this may be a severe airway blockage and help is needed immediately.

If another person is present, ask them to call emergency medical services while care begins.

Provide back blows and abdominal thrusts according to training and local guidance, and keep checking whether the object has been forced out.

If the person becomes unresponsive, begin CPR according to your training and local protocols.
